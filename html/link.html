Link is the Protagonist of "The Legend of Zelda" series. He is a swordsman and a protector of Princess Zelda. He is often the bearer of the Triforce of Courage, which is a sign of him being chosen by the Golden Goddesses.

In Breath of The Wild people will be playing as link throughout the game. In the beginning players watch as he is awakening from a deep sleep in the Shrine of Resurrection. He is then guided by a mysterious voice to take the Sheikah Slate, and embark into the outside world to save hyrule.
